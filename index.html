Day 1 — Chicken + rice Cook once, eat twice: dinner + tomorrow’s work meal
⌄

Work meal — Chicken rice bowl (from yesterday’s dinner)

  • 1 portion of yesterday’s chicken rice bowl from the fridge.
  • Rough guide for 1 portion: chicken 250 g (raw weight for 2 portions), rice 150 g (dry), veg 300 g, olive oil 15 g cooked yesterday – you eat half.
  • If this is your very first day on the plan, just use the same chicken rice bowl you cook for dinner below and pack half for work.

Dinner — Chicken rice bowl (cook 2 portions)

  • Chicken breast 500 g (raw, for 2 big portions)
  • Rice 300 g (dry)
  • Frozen veg 600 g
  • Olive oil 30 g
  • Eat one portion for dinner, cool the second portion and store it in a box for tomorrow’s work meal.

Simple snack — High protein bowl

  • Lactose-free Skyr/Quark 500 g
  • Frozen berries 200 g
  • Honey/jam 25 g
  • Optional: add 30 g whey isolate if protein is low.

Both together (Brajan + Abigail, 1 week)

If you shop together, this is a rough guide for 7 days of dinners + next-day work meals + simple snacks for both of you.

  • Protein: ~3.5–4.0 kg chicken/turkey, ~2.0–2.5 kg lean mince, ~1.0–1.2 kg salmon, 40–50 eggs, ~6–8 kg Skyr/Quark.
  • Carbs: ~2.0–2.5 kg oats, ~2.5–3.5 kg rice (or microwave rice), ~8–12 kg potatoes, 5–8 packs rice cakes, ~25–30 pieces fruit, ~2.5–4.5 kg frozen berries.
  • Fats/extras: 1–2 bottles olive oil, 2 jars peanut butter, ~0.5–1.0 kg nuts.
  • Vegetables: ~4–6 kg frozen veg mix and aim ~0.5–0.6 kg fresh veg per day together (~3.5–4.0 kg/week).

Cheaper swaps (Aldi/Lidl-friendly)

  • Chicken ↔ turkey ↔ lean (pork-free) mince
  • Rice ↔ potatoes ↔ oats
  • Fresh veg ↔ frozen veg (often cheaper + easier)

Weekly adjustment rules (the key to gaining)

  • Weigh daily (morning, after bathroom). Use the 7-day average.
  • Target gain: 0.25–0.5 kg/week.
  • If your 7-day average is flat after 14 days: add +150–200 kcal/day (best: +50 g dry rice OR +60–80 g oats OR +300–400 g potatoes).
  • If you gain >0.6 kg/week for 2 weeks: remove ~150–250 kcal/day (usually from fats: less oil/peanut butter/nuts).
  • If digestion feels off: go laktosefrei only for dairy and reduce bread/pasta for a week (use rice/potatoes/oats instead).
  • Weigh daily (morning, after bathroom). Use the 7-day average.
  • Target gain: 0.2–0.4 kg/week.
  • If your 7-day average is flat after 14 days: add +120–150 kcal/day (best: +30 g dry rice OR +40–60 g oats OR +200–300 g potatoes).
  • If you gain >0.5 kg/week for 2 weeks: remove ~120–150 kcal/day (usually from fats).
  • Lowest effort calorie boost: add 10 g olive oil to meals or an extra snack.

This is a template. Your real “maintenance” can vary—weekly trend adjustment is what makes it work.
