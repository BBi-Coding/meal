Cheaper swaps (Aldi/Lidl-friendly)

  • Chicken ↔ turkey ↔ lean (pork-free) mince
  • Rice ↔ potatoes ↔ oats
  • Fresh veg ↔ frozen veg (often cheaper + easier)

Weekly adjustment rules (the key to gaining)

  • Weigh daily (morning, after bathroom). Use the 7-day average.
  • Target gain: 0.25–0.5 kg/week.
  • If your 7-day average is flat after 14 days: add +150–200 kcal/day (best: +50 g dry rice OR +60–80 g oats OR +300–400 g potatoes).
  • If you gain >0.6 kg/week for 2 weeks: remove ~150–250 kcal/day (usually from fats: less oil/peanut butter/nuts).
  • If digestion feels off: go laktosefrei only for dairy and reduce bread/pasta for a week (use rice/potatoes/oats instead).
  • Weigh daily (morning, after bathroom). Use the 7-day average.
  • Target gain: 0.2–0.4 kg/week.
  • If your 7-day average is flat after 14 days: add +120–150 kcal/day (best: +30 g dry rice OR +40–60 g oats OR +200–300 g potatoes).
  • If you gain >0.5 kg/week for 2 weeks: remove ~120–150 kcal/day (usually from fats).
  • Lowest effort calorie boost: add 10 g olive oil to meals or an extra snack.
